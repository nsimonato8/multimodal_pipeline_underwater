Geographical area:  
- Bay sheltered by Monte Queimado and Monte da Guia.  
- Southwestern coast of the island of Faial, Azores.  

Average depth:  
- Between 8 and 10 meters.  

Water conditions:  
- Winter temperature: 16-18°C.  
- Summer temperature: 22-25°C.  
- High transparency with underwater visibility up to 15 meters.  

Marine characteristics:  
- Presence of schools of fish.  
- Seabed with volcanic rock formations and clear sand.
